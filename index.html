Our Philosophy

From farm to your cup

We believe extraordinary coffee starts long before brewing. We travel to origin farms, build relationships with growers, and roast each batch with the same care and respect those farmers put into their harvest.

  • Direct trade with 12+ smallholder farms worldwide
  • Small-batch roasting, never more than 48hrs old
  • Certified organic and Rainforest Alliance partners
  • Compostable packaging, zero-waste initiative

How We Do It

From bean to brew

🌱
Sourcing
We travel to farms across 3 continents, tasting, selecting, and paying premium prices for exceptional lots.
🔥
Roasting
Roasted in small batches on our vintage Probat drum roaster, developing each bean's full potential.
⚗️
Brewing
Every extraction is dialled in fresh daily. Water temperature, grind size, and time are obsessed over.
☕
Serving
Your cup, made to order. No sitting espresso. Every drink is a fresh, considered act of craft.
